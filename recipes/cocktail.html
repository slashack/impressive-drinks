Washington Apple Cocktail Recipe

Winter with every sip

The combination of whiskey and apple pucker is surprisingly deliciousâ€”it's simple, few ingredients, and the perfect fall/winter cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 11/2 fluid onces cranberry juice
  • 1 fluid ounce sour apple schnapps
  • 1 teaspoon agave nectar
  • ice cubes
  • 2 apple slices for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine whiskey, cranberry juice, apple schnapps, and agave in a cocktail shaker. Fill cocktail shaker with ice and shake well.
  2. Strain into frosted martini glasses and garnish with an apple slice.
